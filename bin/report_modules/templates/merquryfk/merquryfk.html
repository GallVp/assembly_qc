The original Merqury is a collection of R, Java, and shell scripts for producing k-mer analysis plots of genomic sequence data and assemblies with meryl as its core k-mer counter infra-structure. MerquryFK replaces meryl with the FastK k-mer counter suite to considerably speed up analyses.

References:

https://github.com/thegenemyers/MERQURY.FK

Rhie, A., Walenz, B.P., Koren, S. et al. Merqury: reference-free quality, completeness, and phasing assessment for genome assemblies. Genome Biol 21, 245 (2020). doi: 10.1186/s13059-020-02134-9
